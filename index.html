About the Event

What is the GiveHER Effect?

The GiveHER Effect: Powering the Future of AI and Robotics is an empowering gathering that celebrates the role of women in shaping the future of emerging technologies.

This event highlights how women in AI and robotics uplift one another through mentorship, shared knowledge, and meaningful opportunities. Grounded in the principle of "Give to Gain," the initiative fosters a culture of paying it forward.

By bringing together aspiring technologists, professionals, and advocates, the event aims to cultivate inclusive spaces, strengthen networks, and inspire the next generation of women leaders in tech.

Our Objectives

  • 01 Empower women in AI & robotics by showcasing role models
  • 02 Promote mentorship and knowledge-sharing
  • 03 Build inclusive, supportive networks
  • 04 Inspire careers in emerging technologies
  • 05 Encourage a "pay-it-forward" culture in tech

Getting There

Commute Guide to Whitecloak (WCHQ)

20th Floor, Strata 2000, F. Ortigas Jr. Rd., San Antonio, Pasig City

Option 1: MRT-3

  • Ride MRT Line 3
  • Drop-off: Ortigas Station
  • Walk: SM Megamall → Emerald Ave → F. Ortigas Jr. Rd.
Sample: North Ave / Cubao → Ortigas Station → Walk to Strata 2000

Option 2: EDSA Carousel

  • Ride EDSA Carousel
  • Drop-off: Ortigas Bus Stop
  • Walk toward Robinsons Galleria / Megamall → then to Strata 2000
Sample: Monumento / PITX → Ortigas Bus Stop → Walk to Strata 2000

Option 3: UV Express / Jeepney

  • Ride UV/jeep going to: Ortigas Center, Megamall, or Robinsons Galleria
  • Drop-off: SM Megamall or Robinsons Galleria
  • Walk or take a short ride to Strata 2000
Sample: Antipolo → Megamall → Walk to Strata 2000  |  Pasig Palengke → Ortigas → Walk/ride

Option 4: Grab / Taxi

  • Book via Grab app
  • Pin: "Strata 2000, F. Ortigas Jr. Rd., Pasig"
  • or search "Whitecloak WCHQ"
Sample: From home / condo / school → Direct to Strata 2000

Final Landmark

Building: Strata 2000

Nearest: SM Megamall & Robinsons Galleria

Go to: 20th Floor – Whitecloak (WCHQ)

Extra Tips

  • Leave early to avoid heavy traffic in Ortigas
  • Use Google Maps / Waze — search "Whitecloak WCHQ"
  • Ask guards for Strata 2000 if unsure

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

1

What is The GiveHER Effect: Powering the Future of AI and Robotics?

This event is a gathering that celebrates and empowers women in AI and robotics. It focuses on mentorship, innovation, and inclusive leadership while promoting a "Give to Gain" mindset—encouraging participants to support and uplift one another.

2

Who can attend the event?

The event is open to:

  • Students in AI, IT, Engineering, and related fields
  • Young professionals in tech
  • Women in technology communities
  • Industry experts and mentors
  • Partner organizations and invited guests
3

Do I have to be a woman to join?

No. While the event centers on empowering women, it welcomes all individuals who support inclusivity and are interested in AI, robotics, and mentorship.

4

What can participants expect from the event?

Participants can look forward to:

  • Inspiring talks from women leaders in AI and robotics
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities
  • Knowledge-sharing sessions
  • Discussions on career growth and leadership in tech
5

Where will the event be held?

The event will take place at:

White Cloak
20th Floor, Strata 2000, F. Ortigas Jr. Rd, San Antonio, Pasig, Metro Manila

6

Is there a registration fee?

The event is completely free, but registration is required as slots may be limited.

7

Will food be provided?

Light snacks and refreshments will be provided for attendees. Full meals will not be served.

8

How can I register?

Participants can register through the official event registration link (to be provided by organizers). Early registration is recommended.

9

Will there be networking opportunities?

Yes. The event is designed to foster meaningful connections among attendees, mentors, and industry professionals.

10

Can organizations partner or sponsor the event?

Yes. Organizations interested in partnerships or sponsorships may contact the event organizers for collaboration opportunities.

11

What should I bring to the event?

  • Valid ID (if required)
  • Notebook or device for taking notes
  • Business cards (optional, for networking)
  • An open mindset ready to learn and connect
12

Will certificates be provided?

Certificates of participation may be given to attendees after the event.
